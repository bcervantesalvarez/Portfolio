This policy details how personal and non-personal data is gathered, stored, and utilized on this site under a transparent, user-focused approach. Anonymous usage data helps improve navigation and content, while ensuring minimal disruption to your browsing. AI-assisted elements reflect thoughtful final edits, so if you’re curious about the process or have concerns, you’re encouraged to reach out.

My Website Policy

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I want to be fully transparent about how I collect, store, and use information to keep improving the site.

The goal of this policy is to inform you about the nature of the data gathered, whether you choose to provide it directly (e.g., via contact forms) or it’s collected automatically (e.g., analytics). By clarifying these points, I hope you can navigate and enjoy the site with peace of mind.

Transparency

Your privacy matters to me. This policy outlines how my portfolio gathers data—both automatically (e.g., usage metrics) and voluntarily (e.g., you submitting a form). It also covers how that information is used to enhance your experience.

Scope: This policy applies solely to this website and doesn’t extend to external platforms or services you might access through hyperlinks. Once you leave this site, any interaction is bound by that external platform’s privacy rules.

What Data Is Collected?

My data collection can be categorized into two types: anonymous usage data and optional personal data you choose to provide.

  • Pages Visited: The specific pages you explore, helping me see which sections are popular or may need improvement.
  • Time Spent: Average duration on each page to gauge how engaging or valuable the content is.
  • Browser & Device Info: Basic data about your device type, operating system, and browser—this helps me optimize layout and performance.
  • Voluntary Information: Anything you might type into forms, such as your name, email, or message content, if you contact me directly.

Google Analytics is used to collect anonymous metrics. No personally identifiable information (PII) is automatically gathered by my analytics unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via contact forms).

How Is This Data Used?

I use anonymous usage data to understand which areas of the site resonate with visitors. For example:

  • Enhancing navigation so frequently visited sections are easier to access and explore
  • Creating or refining content in areas where users show high engagement or interest
  • Diagnosing performance bottlenecks or design issues on specific pages

Your data is never sold or passed to third parties beyond the analytics processors that supply aggregated metrics. Where personal data is shared voluntarily, such as in a contact form, it’s strictly used to respond to your inquiries or feedback.

Cookie Usage

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that enable better functionality and anonymous statistical analysis. By default, this site primarily employs cookies for analytics. If you choose to block cookies within your browser, the core features of the site will remain accessible, but certain analytics or performance enhancements may not function as intended.

Any cookies used here do not collect PII but rather facilitate session continuity and usage metrics. You remain in control: you can delete existing cookies or prevent new ones from being set at any time.

Your Choices

You have several ways to manage your data and privacy preferences:

  • Cookie Consent Banner: If provided, allows you to opt in or out of certain tracking.
  • Browser Extensions: Google Analytics Opt-out or similar tools can block analytics tracking.
  • Browser Settings: Adjust your settings to disable cookies altogether.
  • Personal Data Requests: If you submit personal info (like through a contact form), you can request its deletion or modification at any time by contacting me directly.

I encourage you to choose whichever methods align best with your comfort level regarding data collection and personalization.

Contact Me

Got questions, concerns, or suggestions related to how data is managed on this site? Fill out the form below, and I’ll do my best to respond.

Constructive feedback about improving this policy is always welcome!
