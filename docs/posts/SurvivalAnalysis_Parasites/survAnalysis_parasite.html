Conclusion

Main findings were:

\1. a strong effect of temperature across all

pesticide trials – higher temperatures caused

cercariae to cease swimming activity earlier

\2. The pesticides were directly toxic to cercariae

to some degree

\3. Important to conduct toxicity studies at

multiple temperatures
